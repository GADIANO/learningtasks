My Favorite Dishes

NUMBER 1

Pork Sinigang

Sinigang is a Filipino sour soup dish that is prepared with tamraind then with meats like pork, beef, chicken, fish and prawns. It is also prepared with different vegetables such as kangkong, okra, radish, string beans, tomatoes and eggplant. I love this dish becuase of its sour soup with the mix of the meats and different vegetables.

NUMBER 2

Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is a filipino dish made by braising chicken legs in sauce made up of vinegar, soy sauce and garlic.I love this dish beacuse it is salty,garlicky and slightly sweet and spicy.

NUMBER 3

Laswa

Laswa is an ilonggo vegetable soup, this dish is made with different vegetables such as, okra, string beans, eggplants, squash, and is usually made with bagoong or meats such as shrimp or pork. I love the blend of the different vegetables and the meat in this dish.

NUMBER 4

Lasagna

Lasagna is an italian dish made with pasta, ground meat, different kinds of cheese, bechamel and tomato sauces. I love this dish because of its exquisite taste because of the different sauces and the scrumptious pasta that is the main ingredient of this dish.

NUMBER 5

Pork Siomai

Pork Siomai is a type of Chinese dumpling, It is usually served as a dim sum snack. Its main ingredients are seasoned ground pork, wonton wrapper, minced carrots and onions. I love it when its served with chili garlic oil and soy sauce and drizzled with calamansi.

NUMBER 6

Dried Fish or Tuyo

Dried Fish also known as, Daing, Tuyo or Bilad are usually gutted, salted, sun and air dried. There are some that are split open or left as whole, there are some that are boneless. I enjoy this dish with spicy vinegar and is best paired with eggs for breakfast.

NUMBER 7

Salted Duck Egg

Salted Duck Eggs are preserved by soaking duck eggs in brine or packing each egg in damp, salted charcoal. I love this dish for its saltiness and how it can be a a great combo with rice.

NUMBER 8

Chicken Nuggets

Chicken Nuggets are made with a small piece of a deboned chicken that is breaded or battered then deep fried or baked. Chicken Nuggets that I usually eat are from Mcdonald's and is my go to comnfort food.

NUMBER 9

Tortang Talong

Tortang Talong or Eggplant Omelette, made by grilling the eggplant then soaked in an egg bath then fried. It is a popular Filipino dish and is a very scrumptious and fulfilling dish. I love this dish because it is fairly easy to cook and is very delicious.

NUMBER 10

Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a delicious and easy to cook dish with a rich and creamy sauce and goes well with rice. It is usually prepared with bone-in chicken with the skin on and cooked with different vegetables such as carrots, potatoes and bellpepper. A juicy, rich and creamy dish making it the last thing on my top 10 list.
