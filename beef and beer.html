    Ingredients


    500g of cheap beefs(stweing steak usually is cheap)
    500 mls of real ale
    4 large onions
    2 fat cloves of garlic
    tbsp of plain flour

    Method

    raw beef, roll it in the flour... fry 4-5 pieces at a time in a hot saucepan to seal... put to one side when all done

    cut the onions into quarterd...fry in the same frying pan as the beef... to soak up the juices... towards the of frying the onion(they should nicely brown roung the edges) throw in the smashed up cloves of garlic.

    transfer the beef, onions,garlic to a deep cassrole dish. Stir in the remaining flour pour on the bottle of beer- really, it isn't going to be waisted!!!

    cover, and cook at 150 for about 2 and a half hours... will be gorgeous when is comes out, trust me... serve with mash
